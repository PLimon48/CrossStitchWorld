About Us

When I first started cross stitching, I had no one to help me learn how. I scoured the web for weeks, watching tutorials from obscure channels just to figure out how to french knot and do text, how to make borderns and avoid tearing the floss. I made this website for those who want to learn, those who don't know where to start, what materials to use. This is for you.
